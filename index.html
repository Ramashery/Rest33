Our Story

Authentic Italian Cuisine with a Modern Touch

Founded in 2010 by Chef Marco Bianchi, Aquarella brings the authentic flavors of Italy to Tbilisi. Our passion for traditional recipes combined with innovative techniques creates a dining experience like no other.

We source the finest ingredients directly from Italy and local Georgian producers, ensuring each dish tells a story of quality and craftsmanship.

Our elegant waterfront location provides the perfect backdrop for romantic dinners, family gatherings, and special celebrations.

Marco Bianchi, Executive Chef

Bruschetta al Pomodoro

Classic Italian appetizer featuring toasted artisan bread rubbed with garlic, topped with diced heirloom tomatoes, fresh basil, and a drizzle of premium extra virgin olive oil. Served with a sprinkle of sea salt and cracked black pepper for the perfect balance of flavors.

Truffle Risotto

Our signature risotto prepared with Carnaroli rice, slowly cooked in a rich mushroom broth infused with black truffle. Finished with aged Parmigiano Reggiano and a touch of butter for creamy texture. Garnished with fresh truffle shavings for an exquisite aroma.

Tiramisu Classico

Traditional Italian dessert made with layers of espresso-soaked ladyfingers and a light, creamy mixture of mascarpone cheese, eggs, and sugar. Dusted with cocoa powder for the perfect finish. Our recipe stays true to the authentic Venetian preparation.
